LAMBORGHINI SUPER TROFEO

Grand Final 2022 November 3-6

Portimao Autodrome, Portugal

SUPER TROPHY WORLD FINAL

The Lamborghini World Finals has rounded off every Super Trofeo season since 2013. Each year, drivers from all continental championships go to different tracks and take part in two 50-minute sprint races, where the overall winner. as the Lamborghini World Champion. The Lamborghini World Finals is not only the biggest racing weekend of the year, but also a true festival of motorsports that attracts fans from all over the world.

The owners' parade, previews and paddock entertainment for fans are just some of the events organized as part of the event.

SUPER TROPHY TEAMS

All of the cars that compete in the Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships are driven by private teams that use their expertise to fine-tune them for the drivers. For each Lamborghini Super Trofeo championships there are driver and team rankings.

Drivers and teams participating in the Continental Series compete against each other every year in the Lamborghini Grand Final,the event that concludes the Super Trofeo sporting season. Two qualifying sessions and two races determine the world champions of the series. Each year the Grand Final is held at a different track, and in past editions it has touchedEuropean, Asian and American continents.

PORTIMAO TRACK

The Autodromo Internacional do Algarve is located in the region of the same name in the south of Portugal, near the city of Portimão.Opened in October 2008, the racing complex is one of the most modern in Europe, spread over an area of 300 hectares among the hills surrounding the Portuguese city and the length of the track itself is 4,684 meters.

SUPER TROFEO EVO

The new Huracán Super Trofeo EVO achieves even greater heights than its illustrious predecessor thanks to a complete completely redesigned aerodynamics. The Huracán Super Trofeo EVO is the ideal launching pad for all drivers who Want to begin a career in GT class racing.

The Huracán EVO is the evolution of the most successful Lamborghini with a V10 engine. As a result of the fine-tuning and refinement of existing features, combined with new design solutions that enhance performance, the car stands out for its ability to anticipate and satisfy driver behavior, expectations and desires.

Among the most obvious changes to the Lamborghini Huracan Super Trofeo EVO's bodywork are a rear spoiler on the rear hood and The upper air intake, which makes it instantly recognizable to onlookers.
